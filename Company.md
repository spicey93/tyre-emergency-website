# Company Profile & Copywriting Guide: Tyre Emergency Ltd

This document is the "Source of Truth" for all website copy and brand communications. Use these specific phrases and values to maintain a consistent, reassuring presence.

---

## 1. Core Identity
* **Company Name:** Tyre Emergency Ltd
* **Tagline:** Need a tyre urgently? Call Tyre Emergency
* **Mission Statement:** To provide a fast, expert emergency tyre service at a fair price, ensuring no driver is left stranded
* **Vision Statement:** To be the Midlands' premier rapid-response tyre fleet, reaching any driver in 60 minutes or less

## 2. Target Audience (The "Who")
* **Primary Persona:** Time-poor drivers (parents, commuters, professionals) who need an immediate solution to resume their day
* **Pain Points:** 
    * Lack of a spare wheel
    * Slow or unhelpful traditional breakdown cover
    * Safety concerns while stuck roadside
* **Desired Outcomes:** Rapid return to the road with zero hassle

## 3. Brand Voice & Tone
* **Personality Traits:** Simple, friendly, reassuring, and professional
* **Voice Guidelines:**
    * **Do:** Use active, "rescue-oriented" verbs; speak directly to the customer's safety; keep instructions clear and brief
    * **Don't:** Use alarmist language, overcomplicate the technical process, or use industry jargon that might confuse a stressed driver

## 4. Key Value Propositions (The "Why Us")
* **Speed:** Industry-leading average ETA of 30 minutes
* **Availability:** True 24/7 emergency coverage—we never clock off
* **Experience:** 10+ years of specialist expertise in mobile tyre solutions
* **Trust:** REACT Licensed for high-speed roadside safety and a perfect 5.0 Google rating

## 5. Product/Service Breakdown
### Emergency Tyre Replacement
* **Description:** 24/7 roadside response for blowouts and flat tyres
* **Feature vs. Benefit:** 30-minute average response time means less time spent in a dangerous roadside environment

### Mobile Tyre Fitting
* **Description:** Professional tyre fitting at your home or workplace
* **Feature vs. Benefit:** We bring the garage to you, eliminating travel costs and saving your valuable time

### Mobile Tyre Repair
* **Description:** On-site puncture repairs to get you moving without a full replacement
* **Feature vs. Benefit:** Mobile service removes the risk of driving on a flat to reach a garage

### Locking Wheel Nut Removal
* **Description:** Specialist removal of stubborn or damaged locking nuts and bolts
* **Feature vs. Benefit:** High success rate across all makes and models with minimal risk of wheel damage

## 6. Social Proof & Authority
* **Key Stats:** 3,000+ drivers rescued; 450+ 5-star Google reviews
* **Certifications:** REACT Licensed (Motorway & Roadside Safety)
* **Core Philosophy:** Speed, safety, and efficiency—delivered at a fair price

## 7. Calls to Action (CTAs)
* **Primary (Urgent):** "Stuck now? Call us for a 30-min ETA"
* **Secondary (Enquiry):** "Get a quick quote online"

## 8. Boilerplate (About Us)
Tyre Emergency Ltd was founded by Dean Spicer, an industry expert with over a decade of experience in mobile tyre solutions. From his early days fitting tyres alongside his father in Dorset to managing a rapid-response fleet in the Midlands, Dean has built a business centered on speed and safety. With a perfect 5.0 Google rating and REACT-licensed technicians, Tyre Emergency provides 24/7 roadside assistance, locking wheel nut removal, and mobile fitting, ensuring drivers get back on the road safely and efficiently.
